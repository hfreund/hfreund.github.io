Courageous Sailing

Website Design & Build

Courageous Sailing uses their website for four audiences: Parents Youth Program sailors, Lessons & Memberships, Events, and Donors. Working with the Courageous staff, I redesigned the website to include an ecommerce solution (WooCommerce).

Role

Fast Access

The revised home page provides newcommers to the organizaiton with a quick overview of the Courgaeous mission and sets the tone for the visitor. At the same time, the home page needs to provide current members, parents, program participants, and active donors or community members with quick access to information that is relevant to them.

I defined a multi-tiered navigation that includes:

  • Quick access to commonly used pages (like weather, boat reservations, and donations)
  • A mega menu with key information given a featured spot
  • Sub modules below the homepage hero to feature seasonally rotated promotions, events, or programs.

Youth Programs

The primary audience of the Youth Program section is parents, although there is also a lot of donor and board member traffic to this part of the website. Courageous offers a few main programs across four sites, so our first goal was to make it easy for parents to find age-appropriate information at the right location for their child. We also wanted to provide a really strong overview of the types of children served, how programs have impact, and what Courageous can do to help families who need assistence either financially or logistically (i.e. with transportation).

Adult & Family Sailing

For new and current students and members, this part of the website is focused on driving membership renewal and course enrollment. There are a few top level pages to give an overview of what to expect, but the majority of content lies in the ecommerce section.

WooCommerce

For a long time Courageous functioned with an ad-hoc system comprosed of PayPal buttons for purchasing courses and memberships. This was brittle, required a lot of staff time to maintain, and had a poor customer experience. I implemented a totally new ecommerce solution with WooCommerce, enabling online reservations, product tracking, automated communication, and better management of product inventory, sale pricing, and deals.

Events

Courageous hosts a few signature events each year and also rents space for private functions (like weddings and launch parties) for up to 600 people. For Courageous events, I integrated Eventbrite ticketing with a custom layout to promote the event and give credit to sponsors.
