Courageous Sailing

Brand Identity and Marketing

The organization had received an in-kind rebrand a couple of years before I arrived, however brand assets were organized poorly and not well defined. I collected all the ad-hoc brand variations and developed a simple style guide and library of assets for the team to use. Then I worked with different team leaders to design marketing materials that supported the emergent brand.

Style Guide & Logo Use

Courageous operates with a small core staff, some part time staff, volunteers, and board members, therefore, presenting a consistent voice and tone throughout all internal and external communications is a challenge. My aim with this style guide was threefold:

  • Simplicity - Most people aren't going to read a 30 page style guide if they are in a hurry.
  • Adaptability - The standards had to work for print, web, email, and video.
  • Portability - No licensed fonts or plugins required - anyone can use it.
