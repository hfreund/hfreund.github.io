Courageous Sailing

Brand Identity

The organization received an in-kind rebrand before I arrived, however brand assets were not well organized or defined. I collected all the ad-hoc variations and developed a simple style guide and library of assets for the team to use. Then I worked with different team leaders to design marketing materials that supported the emergent brand identity.

Style Guide & Logo Use

Courageous operates with a small core staff, some part time staff, volunteers, and board members, therefore, presenting a consistent voice and tone throughout all internal and external communications is a challenge. My aim with this style guide was threefold:

  • Simplicity - A one-page style guide would be better received by non-designers
  • Adaptability - The standards had to work for print, web, email, and video
  • Portability - No licensed fonts or plugins required - anyone can use it

Print Marketing

I rebuilt all of the marketing materials which were both out of date and out of step with each other and the brand standard. The requirements were to be flexible enough to be used as an in person sales tool or a take-away item. They also had to be strong enough to stand on their own while maintaining strong ties to the overall brand identity.

Annual Report

As the first significant outward-facing Annual Report the organization had ever published, I had both a lot of freedom to move the design in a specific direction. However, this was a major undertaking that had to be produced in around three weeks, so some self-imposed restriction on layout complexity was necessary to ensure an on-time delivery.

There were a lot of key stakeholders in this project which meant the review window was packed full of changes. Given the rapid turn-around required, I enforced strict timelines and expectations for reviews to keep the process moving efficiently.

Since this was the first Annual Report, I felt it was necessary to set a particular tone that was different from any other correspondence the organization had sent in the past.
